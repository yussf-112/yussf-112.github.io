in the presence of spring

i do remember you,
a familiar face,
that reminds me of "home"
a sweet warm place

and spring called your name

and u answered the call
u embraced the end of winter and fall

O' spring, come!

for a love
that is yet to end


the sight of cherry blossoms
the sound of sea's tranquile waves
the smell of a garden after rain
the feel of ur soft, warm hands...

it was u, beneath the clouds?
or perhaps...
u were spring.
