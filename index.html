ENTRY VII :

the light breaks as the man enter the room.
white coats the insides of the room, resembling blood in its appearence.
hell and heaven break loose in the man's chaotic mind, and his eyes never seem to focus on what's in front of him; he's looking blankly at the exit door, contemplating, lost in a maze of his own design.
he doesn't seem to feel the time flow, he doesn't seem to feel his blood flow.
hours pass, and his muscles cannot continue to hold the unholy weight of his thoughts, and so every fiber, every tissue in his body starts to tear down.
yet, the man doesn't mind his own collapsing, and continues to gaze into the abyss.
the abyss.

he is in fact. the abyss.
his eyes curse him actively, as the tears start to tear his own face.
the tears roll down his face, trying to escape the reality of their existence.
to remember, he is trying.
to forget, he is avoiding.

he thinks... and the thought will linger on.

damned is man.
damned is man to think.

and then... the light pierces his heart.
for a moment, he felt Life blooming in his chest, as Death watches the chaos unleashing in his eyes...
and then the man died in grief.
